About Lion Of Judah Schools

Lion of Judah Schools was founded in 2017 by the school Director Ap. Pr.Saul Yawe with an aim of providing accessible and quality education to students in the community. Over the years, it has grown into a respected institution known for Academic performance, God-fearing and discipline. This school focuses on academic excellence, discipline and character development to prepare learners for higher education and future careers.

Our Mission

To raise God-fearing, academically sound, and self-reliant individuals

Our Vision

To provide top quality education that raises God-fearing, academically sound & self-reliant individuals

Our Core Values

  • God-fearing
  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Discipline
  • Academic Excellence
  • Teamwork
  • Innovation
